MALDIVES

The Maldives, a tropical nation in the Indian Ocean, is renowned for its stunning natural beauty, vibrant marine life, and luxurious resorts, making it a popular tourist destination. Its appeal lies in the combination of idyllic beaches, turquoise lagoons, colorful coral reefs, and exciting water sports, attracting both honeymooners and families.

Snorkeling

The Maldives offers a wide array of water sports activities, from serene kayaking and snorkeling to thrilling jet skiing and parasailing. Popular options include scuba diving, windsurfing, stand-up paddleboarding, wakeboarding, and catamaran sailing. Visitors can also enjoy unique experiences like flyboarding, underwater scooters
